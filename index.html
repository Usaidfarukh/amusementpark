Merciado Amusement Park is a place of fun, excitement, and wonder — designed for visitors of all ages.

Our park combines thrilling rides, beautiful scenery, and family-friendly entertainment for unforgettable experiences.An amusement park is a dedicated, permanent destination designed to offer a wide variety of entertainment for guests of all ages. Far more organized and elaborate than simple playgrounds or traveling carnivals, these parks feature an array of attractions, including thrilling roller coasters and family-friendly rides, complemented by themed shows, dining options, and carnival games. Modern amusement parks often differentiate themselves as "theme parks" by meticulously designing every element—from architecture to staff costumes—to create a unified, immersive environment, allowing visitors to step into a fantasy world and create unforgettable, joy-filled memories with every visit.
