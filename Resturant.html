Our Dining Destinations

The Fun Feast Arena

The Fun Feast is our largest and most popular dining spot, offering all-day quick service. Here you'll find classic amusement park favorites: **gourmet burgers, crispy fries, hot dogs, and freshly made pizza slices**. Perfect for families who need a fast, delicious, and satisfying meal without missing out on park time. Plenty of indoor and outdoor seating is available!

Adventure Bites Cafe

For those looking for lighter fare or a refreshing break, the Adventure Bites Cafe is the ideal spot. We specialize in fresh **salads, wraps, sandwiches, and specialty coffee drinks**. This cafe offers a relaxing atmosphere away from the hustle, complete with comfy seating and free Wi-Fi. Don't forget to try our freshly baked pastries and signature ice cream sundaes!

Dining Services & Info
Allergy & Dietary Needs

We take dietary needs seriously. Please inform our staff about any **allergies or special requests** upon ordering.

Indoor & Outdoor Seating

Enjoy your meal in our **air-conditioned dining hall** or relax with **park views** at our shaded outdoor tables.

Operating Hours

Our main restaurants are open **30 minutes before park opening** and **close 30 minutes after** the last ride.
