About us

Our Story

The Beginning of an Adventure

**Merciado Amusement Park** was founded in **1985** with a simple, profound dream: to create a destination where families and friends could escape the everyday and immerse themselves in pure joy and unforgettable adventure. Starting with a few classic, beloved rides, our park quickly grew into the expansive and thrilling destination you experience today.

Our park is built on a foundation of **nostalgic charm and modern thrills**. We are relentlessly committed to innovation, stringent safety standards, and delivering experiences that evoke wonder in guests of all ages. Every carefully designed zone, every attraction, and every event at Merciado is crafted to be a chapter in your family's happiest memories.
